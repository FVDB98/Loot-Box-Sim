CSGO Case Simulator

Intro

In Counter Strike: Global Offensive (CSGO), there are a number of different cases which each hold a myriad of different gun skins and items. The value of these can range from $0.05 to well over $1000s. How will you fare?

Items

To keep things simple, the special items you will be looking to get are knives or gloves. Below is an image showing some of these items:

Probability

Certain items can be found in different cases. For ease of use, this simulator acts as if all items are in 1 big case. The following probability for items are:

  • Navy Blue (Rare) – 79.92%
  • Purple (Mythical) – 15.98%
  • Hot Pink (Legendary) – 3.2%
  • Red (Ancient) – 0.64%
  • Gold (Exceedingly Rare) – 0.26%

Cost

Keys for different cases can range in value. For ease of use once again, we will be pricing each key (case opening) as a flat $2.

How to Play

By clicking on the 'open case' button, you will be simulating the odds of a single case opening. Be wary, you could have to press this button over 1000 times.

By clicking on the 'auto open' button, the simulator will run until you unlock an Exceedingly Rare item.

Good Luck

Now you know what you are looking for, see how much money it could cost you to uncase some of the rarest Gold Items (Exceedingly Rare) that lurk within these cases.
