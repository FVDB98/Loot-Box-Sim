CSGO Case Simulator

Intro

In Counter Strike: Global Offensive (CSGO), there are a number of different cases which contain different virtual items. The value of these items can range from $0.05 to well over $1000s.

Items

To keep things simple, the special items you will be looking to get are knives or gloves. Below are images showing some of these items:

Each item comes in a certain condition too. In order of most valuable, these are:

  1. Factory new
  2. Minimum wear
  3. Field-tested
  4. Battlescarred

As a result, if one unlocks 2 of the same item but one is 'Factory New' and the latter is 'Field-tested', the former is more valuable.

Probability

Certain items can be found in different cases. For ease of use, this simulator acts as if all items are in 1 big case. The following probability for items are:

  • Navy Blue (Rare): 79.92%
  • Purple (Mythical): 15.98%
  • Hot Pink (Legendary): 3.2%
  • Red (Ancient): 0.64%
  • Gold (Exceedingly Rare): 0.26%

Cost

Keys for different cases can range in value. For ease of use once again, we will be pricing each key (case opening) as a flat $2 USD.

How to Play

By clicking on the 'open case' button, you will be simulating the odds of a single case opening. Be wary, you could have to press this button over 1000 times.

By clicking on the 'auto open' button, the simulator will run automatically until you unlock an Exceedingly Rare item.
